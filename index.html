About AuraRead

This is a Book recommendation System based on your current Mood. Traditional book recommendation systems suggest titles based on genres or popularity, but they often miss out on one critical factor—how you feel. AuraRead not only finds books that spark your interest, but also books that resonate with your current mood. We used web scrapers to extract mood from book descriptions and reviews, integrating these moods into our dataset. Our model uses collaborative filtering, enhanced with matrix factorization techniques, to predict which books might interest you based on your previous interactions and current mood. Happy reading!
